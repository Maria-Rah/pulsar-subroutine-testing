Note on PDOT_DECAY

Why XKDECAY = 0:

The B-field decay is already explicitly calculated through the exponential decay term: B(t) = B‚ÇÄ √ó exp(-t/œÑ). Including an additional PDOT_DECAY term creates double counting of the same physical process. The period derivative from B-field changes is already captured through the time-varying B¬≤ term in the dipole radiation formula.

  • Manchester & Taylor (1977): Pulsars - B-field evolution through exponential decay only
